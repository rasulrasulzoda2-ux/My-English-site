The Past Simple tense is one of the most important tenses in English. It is used to talk about actions or situations that happened at a specific time in the past and are now finished.

üìå When to Use the Past Simple

  • For actions completed at a definite time in the past.
    Example: I finished my homework an hour ago.
  • For a series of completed actions in the past.
    Example: I woke up, brushed my teeth, and went to school.
  • For past habits or states.
    Example: He played the piano when he was a child.

Sentence Structures

Type Structure Example
Affirmative Subject + Past Verb (V2) She visited London last year.
Negative Subject + did not (didn't) + Base Verb (V1) She did not (didn't) go to school yesterday.
Question Did + Subject + Base Verb (V1)? Did they watch the movie?
